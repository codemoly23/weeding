## Key Strategic Insights

1. **"For non-residents" modifier is the differentiator.** Big competitors (ZenBusiness, LegalZoom) target US residents. Adding "for non-residents / foreigners / from Bangladesh" turns high-competition terms into winnable long-tails.

2. **TikTok Shop is a blue ocean right now.** No established service provider has content targeting non-resident TikTok Shop setup. Move fast.

3. **Form 5472 + BOI content converts by fear.** $25,000 penalty risk drives urgent purchases. Medium volume, very high conversion.

4. **Country-specific pages rank in weeks.** Almost zero competition for `US LLC from Bangladesh` style queries. Build 4 dedicated pages.

5. **Amazon reinstatement = highest revenue per visitor.** Suspended sellers are in emergency mode and buy immediately.

6. **Banking is a recurring search.** "How to open US bank account as non-resident" is searched at every stage. Evergreen traffic driver.
